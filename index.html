About Me

My name is Deboleena, and I love to design products for users, to make their life simpler and easier to work on.I have led a variety of projects, ranging from creating a user-friendly travel app to promoting community involvement with a unique surplus food-sharing platform. Furthermore, I have applied my design skills to improve the online presence of a mental health-focused non-profit organization and to enhance the user experience of the Australian defense website. Additionally, I have demonstrated my technical proficiency by independently developing a personal website using HTML, CSS, and Javascript, underscoring my commitment to both design and development.
